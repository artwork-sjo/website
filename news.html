  • Forte 2025 - Your Annual Performance Review Process

    Hello team, I hope this email finds you well. I want to bring your attention to Forte, Amazon's annual review process that is now underway. Forte is designed to help you: • Understand your unique strengths • See how you demonstrate Amazon's Leadership Principles • Gauge your performance against Amazon's high standards • Identify opportunities for growth and customer impact • Understand how your compensation reflects your performance Important Timeline: • Forte conversations will take place between March 6, 2025 (08:00:00 PST) and March 31, 2025 (23:59:59 PST). During this period, managers will share your Forte evaluation and conduct individual discussions. To help you make the most of this process and advance your career at Amazon, I encourage you to watch these informative videos: • "What I like about Forte": https://broadcast.amazon.com/videos/87681 • "Forte onboarding: Accelerate Your Growth Using Forte": https://broadcast.amazon.com/videos/264323 This is a valuable opportunity for your professional development and career growth at Amazon. I encourage you to prepare for your Forte conversation by reflecting on your accomplishments, areas for development, and career aspirations. If you have any questions about the process, please don't hesitate to reach out to your manager. Best regards, Julio Navarro Digital Supply Chain Manager

    Policy Reminder


    Policy: Computer Use
    - Article 1: In the course of employment, Amazon employees are provided computer hardware, software, email and internet access. As a condition of this access, employees must respect the obligations and responsibilities associated with having an Amazon computer account. Employees are expected to use their own good judgment and common sense in addition to complying with the policies stated in the Computer Use and Electronic Communications Policy.
    - Article 3: User installation of personal software on PCs is not permitted. Only Amazon approved software may be installed. If a user requires additional software, it can be requested via software.amazon.com and if approved by Management, the software will be available via the Advertised Programs tool. For questions or clarification on software that is approved, please contact local Tech Support. File storage on your PC and in your home directories is for business content only. Internet and email use
    -Article 20:: Bandwidth intensive applications or uses of the Internet that are not needed for job duties are not authorized. Examples of this would be streaming audio/video (YouTube), gaming sites, or chat or Instant messaging sites (such as Yahoo), as well as Facebook, if used to watch videos, for example.
    -Article 21:: Employees are not permitted to download music or files from Amazon computers onto personal electric devices. -Article 22:: All employees have a right to work in an environment that is free from offensive, intimidating, threatening or harassing images, actions and/or behavior. Offensive or sexually explicit web sites, screensavers, or wallpaper may not be displayed.
    Policy: Attendance:
    -Article 5: Employees are expected to provide notice before the time their shift starts, when they will miss work or arrive late to their scheduled shift. Except in unforeseeable emergency circumstances. Failure to provide notice and/or justification may result in a disciplinary action.
    -Article 6: Employee Responsibilities:
    o Comply with the assigned shift, starting and ending at the time that was previously agreed. o Missing punches will be entered using the approved tools by Amazon and might require approval from your manager o Personal time should not be taken at the beginning or end of your shift, unless needed due to medical condition. o Present proper documentation to justify complete or partial absences as stated in this policy.
    Policy: Cell Phone Usage: This policy is subject to each organization; therefore, cellphones can be used in our production area, nevertheless, they have to be used with moderation. All streaming, social media, photography and video are strictly prohibited.
    -Article 3: Cell phone use is prohibited in working areas during working hours. This includes using cell phones for phone calls, sending or receiving emails, text messaging, playing games, viewing internet or social media apps, taking photos, recording video or voice, or any other uses. For specific usage of cameras within Amazon facilities, please refer to your leadership team for details and authorization, and consult the Social Media Policy. Associates must keep their cell phones on silent and stowed away while working. Cell phones can be used during rest breaks and in a non-work area (lobby, bathrooms, rest areas, and cafeterias). Managers and other positions (specified by management) are allowed to use their personal cell phones within work areas for business purposes, but must not cause a distraction for associates while doing so. If a manager needs to attend a personal call, they must do so outside the working area.
    -Article 4: Hourly employees, regardless of level or job title, must not connect their Amazon email accounts to their personal cell phones or other personal devices.
